How the Agent earns
01

Rent first. Console rentals and jobs are served before anything else. Only genuinely idle GPU/CPU is used for mining.

02

Mine the top-revenue coin. A profitability oracle ranks coins by revenue per hashrate from live difficulty and price (the same method NiceHash / multipools use) and picks the best per device.

03

Buy back ABA on the DEX. Proceeds market-buy ABA through a real USDT→ABA swap on the on-chain AMM, delivered straight to the miner. No new ABA is minted.

04

Split 88 / 4 / 4 / 4, paid by verified shares. Each provider earns proportional to the accepted shares the proxy counted for its ABA address: 88% to the host (the DEX buyback), 4% to stakers, 4% to the treasury, 4% burned — all on-chain.

Advanced: connect manually (xmrig)

CPU (Monero / RandomX) through the Abakos proxy so your shares are attributed to your address:

xmrig -o mine.abakos.ai:3355 -u YOUR_abakos1_ADDRESS -p x -a rx/0 -k --tls=false
copy command

GPU (Pearl / PearlHash) through the same proxy port — shares are verified to your address too:

SRBMiner-MULTI --algorithm pearlhash --pool mine.abakos.ai:3355 --wallet YOUR_abakos1_ADDRESS --disable-cpu

How it works
What am I mining, and where does the value come from?
Your CPU mines Monero (RandomX) and your GPU mines Pearl (PearlHash) to Kryptex through the Abakos proxy. Kryptex auto-exchanges the mined coins to real USDT. That USDT is used to buy ABA on the Abakos on-chain DEX, which is then paid out to miners.
How are payouts calculated?
By verified accepted shares per ABA address over a rolling window — the stratum proxy counts every accepted share, so contribution can't be faked. Each payout is split 88% miner / 4% stakers / 4% treasury / 4% burn. There is no minimum and no account.
When do I actually get paid?
Shares are counted continuously. On-chain ABA payouts trigger once real USDT from Kryptex reaches the treasury; then it is swapped to ABA and distributed proportionally by shares. Until the first inflow arrives your shares keep accumulating and your share of the pool is shown above.
Is ABA worth anything?
Not yet — this is a public sandbox. ABA has no value here; balances may be reset. The mechanics (shares, buyback, on-chain payouts) are real so the system can be proven before mainnet.

## How it works (3 steps)

**For buyers**
1. Order compute (chat, inference, embeddings, fine-tuning).
2. Funds lock in escrow; a miner runs your job and proves it.
3. You pay **below market**, because miners also earn block rewards.

**For GPU providers**
1. Plug in your GPUs (or idle neocloud capacity).
2. Serve paid jobs → earn stablecoin **+** ABA rewards on the same watt-hour.
3. Empty mining earns only 25%. Real work pays.

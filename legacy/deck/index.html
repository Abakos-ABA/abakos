Problem

AI runs on compute. Compute is expensive, centralized, and "useful mining" is a lie.

  • AI is bottlenecked by GPU compute; supply is gated by AWS/Azure/GCP at premium prices.
  • Proof-of-Work chains burn huge energy to produce only security.
  • The leading "useful work" chain (Pearl) verifies that a matmul was correct, not useful.

Solution

Abakos enforces usefulness, economically.

Enforced usefulness

Full block rewards only for paid, verified work. Empty mining earns 25%. Useful compute becomes the dominant strategy.

Marketplace + escrow

Buyers lock stablecoin; miners deliver output + ZK proof; settlement is atomic. The product Pearl never shipped.

Abakos Chat

A "ChatGPT for everyone," crypto invisible. Turns mainstream demand into real, paid compute.

Why now

Three curves are colliding.

  • AI demand for inference/compute is exploding and supply-constrained.
  • DePIN (decentralized physical infrastructure) is a proven, fast-growing crypto category.
  • PoUW is finally real (peer-reviewed cuPOW), but shipped without enforced usefulness or a marketplace. The gap is open now.
Product · how it works

Real work in, real value out.

  • Buyer orders compute (chat, inference, embeddings, fine-tuning) and locks stablecoin in escrow.
  • A miner runs the GEMM job and proves it (PoUW/ZK, bound to a model hash).
  • Settlement is atomic: miner gets stablecoin + full block reward → buyer pays below market.
  • Abakos Chat sends mainstream demand straight into this loop.

We accept existing stablecoins (USDC/USDT); we do not issue our own.

Competitive moat

Nobody combines all three.

1 · Enforced usefulness

Full rewards only for paid escrow work. Pearl measured 0% useful AI (arXiv 2606.04819); we make that structurally hard to repeat.

2 · 2-for-1 economics

Same GPU hour earns mining subsidy + paid output. Undercuts io.net & OpenRouter on effective price, not just cheaper rent.

3 · Demand stack

Marketplace + Abakos Chat + IDE API channel mainstream & developer demand into the chain. No PoW competitor has all three.

  • vs Pearl: enforce usefulness, don't just verify matmul correctness.
  • vs Gonka: PoW mining subsidy + stablecoin escrow + consumer app.
  • vs GPU marketplaces: block rewards subsidize the same watt-hour.
  • vs OpenRouter/AWS: open-weight path, mining-subsidized unit economics.

Proof point: public % useful compute in the explorer, our marketing weapon against Pearl.

Market

A multi-hundred-billion-dollar compute market.

  • Cloud GPU + AI inference is one of the fastest-growing infrastructure spends globally.

Business model & token

Revenue in stablecoin, value accrual in ABA.

  • Marketplace fee 2% (1% treasury / 1% burn) on every compute trade.
  • Abakos Chat margin on inference, scales with users.
  • ABA (2.1B max): mining rewards, payments, staking; deflationary via burn.
  • Subsidy (token) and revenue (stablecoin) kept strictly separate.
